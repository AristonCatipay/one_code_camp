2. Tick the `I accept the terms and License Agreement` then click the `Next` button.
3. Click the `Complete` button. Click the `Install MongoD as a Service` then click the `Next` button.
4. Tick the `Install MongoDB Compass` then click the `Next` button.
5. Click the `Finish` button.
